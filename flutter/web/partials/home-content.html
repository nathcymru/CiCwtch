Why the name “CiCwtch”?

“Ci” is Welsh for dog. “Cwtch” is one of those Welsh words that does more than one job: a cuddle, a safe place, a feeling of comfort. That is the point of the brand. CiCwtch is meant to feel supportive and steady — a platform that helps dog walkers feel more in control, more organised and less alone with the admin side of the business.
