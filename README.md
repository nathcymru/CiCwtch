## 🐶 About CiCwtch

CiCwtch is a structured digital platform designed for small dog walking and pet care businesses across the UK and Europe.

It exists to provide dependable systems for professionals and reassuring simplicity for families. The focus is long-term architectural stability, disciplined engineering, and clarity over feature bloat.

CiCwtch is engineered deliberately. Stability and coherence matter more than speed.

## 🤝 Collaboration Model

CiCwtch is **not open source**.

However, structured collaboration is welcomed under a formal process.

Contributors may:

- Volunteer (approved contributors)
- Be contracted/procured for defined work packages
- Participate via GitHub Issues and Pull Requests once authorised

Before contributing, review:

- [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)
- [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md)
- [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
- [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md)

All contributions are subject to approval and may be incorporated into commercial versions of CiCwtch under the terms defined in CONTRIBUTING.md.
